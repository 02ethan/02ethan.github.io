Rare Cat Capsules

Rare Cat Capsules can be used to acquire rare, super rare, and uber rare cats. Uber rare cats are the best because they have lots more health than regular cats and higher damage to different types of enemies.

Acquiring Cats

When opening rare cat capsules, you can get different cats than what you start with(cat, wall cat, axe cat, etc.) These new cats deal more damage and have more health. You can purchase rare cat capsules with cat food or when they have an event. During these events, you can get rare cat tickets to open capsules and get cats.

Regular Cat Capsules

Every day each player receives one Cat Ticket to find regular cats and upgrade them. If you do not want to upgrade your base level cats, you can destroy them for XP to upgrade other cats.
